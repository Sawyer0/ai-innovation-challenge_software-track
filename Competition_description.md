The event is hosted by BMCC Technology Learning Community project and BMCC Tech Innovation Hub Project
Students will work together to make Social Good projects using AI and following Public Interest Technology Framework
Empowers CUNY students from all disciplines & majors to prepare for the workforce
Provide a learning environment for all students to learn teamwork, leadership
Build real-world solutions for social good
Compete for prizes, build your network, and make new friends
The purpose of this event is to inspire CUNY students to learn AI and work in a collaborative setting to solve real-world challenges at CUNY.
It is a 2 day long event where students will create teams, design and build an AI powered project, and pitch their ideas to Industry judges for cash prizes at the end of the second day.
The competitive tracks are:
AI No-Code 
building web apps or services; no coding or AI  experience necessary
AI-Data Science (coding required)
Using real-world data to analyze and solve problems; create a presentation of your results and build a product using your data
AI Software - Agentic AI (coding required)
advanced AI project building; for engineers or AI aficionados 

Each project should be focused on one of these themes for Social Good:
🌍 AI Innovation Challenge: Tech for Change Theme Tracks

🌱 AI for Environmental Sustainability
⚖️ AI for Justice, Policy & Equity
🧠 AI for Health & Public Good
🏫 AI for Accessible & Inclusive Education
🏙️ AI for Housing & Urban Communities
🤝 AI for Student Community & Digital Access
🔬 AI for Science, Data & Public Innovation
